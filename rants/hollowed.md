title: hollowed
---



craftings of love, made from delicate clay
scorched in heat, born with savaging burns  

not to torment , not to hear screeching chimes  
Mold to hard, yet of tender heart  

a wishful grace, cast the potter  
Who love the same, forgive the same  

Borne no sins,  
though born hollowed, fill with love  

Of all things crafted  
not fall for the graceful craft  
look deep for the life it holds  

in the end I must bid and so everyone  
let not filled with grief and guilt  
let not hold vindictive grudges  
of those unfilled and unsolved  

all must turn to mud, and born hollowed once again
