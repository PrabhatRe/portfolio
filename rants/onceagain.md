title: Once Again
---

Once again,  
i wish i could talk to you  
on a new day, under new light, with a new shade  

Once again,  
memories come back, writhed in pain  
wrecking those broken, those healed alike  

sins i bore, for the journey that came to an end  
for things that have happened, i beg forgiveness  

Once again,  
i wish i could talk to you  
not today, not tomorrow, for longer than i know
